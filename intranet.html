Intranet

An intranet is a private network used by an organization. Its primary purpose is to help employees communicate, collaborate, and share information and tools securely within the organization. Unlike the public Internet, an intranet is restricted to authorized users only.

Intranets often use the same technologies as the Internet, such as web servers and TCP/IP, but they are protected by firewalls and other security measures to prevent external access.
