Web 2.0

Web 2.0, also known as the "participatory Web" or "social Web," refers to websites that emphasize user-generated content, ease of use, participatory culture, and interoperability (i.e., compatible with other products, systems, and devices) for end users.

Examples of Web 2.0 include social media sites (Facebook, Twitter), video sharing sites (YouTube), wikis (Wikipedia), and blogs. It shifted the web from static pages to dynamic, interactive platforms where anyone can contribute content.
